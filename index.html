How I think

  • Clarity over comfort. I'd rather know the hard truth than believe a comfortable lie.
  • Systems over heroics. Good design means the system works, not that someone had to save it.
  • Decisions over discussions. Thinking is valuable. Deciding is where it counts.
  • Substance over performance. I'm not interested in looking smart. I'm interested in being useful.
