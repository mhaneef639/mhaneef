How I think

  • Clarity over comfort. I prefer to know the truth, bitter and difficult as it is, over living convinced by a comfortable lie.
  • Good systems beat heroics. A well-designed system beats heroic attempts to save what can be saved. Good design means the system works.. it doesn't need rescuing and doesn't live in a state of chronic emergency.
  • Substance over appearance. I don't care about appearing smart. I care about being useful, building an effective system, and having its beauty reflect from the inside (its truth) to the outside (its appearance).
