Mohammed Salama, Creative Direction, Zanzibar Food Episode

Salama saw something in me before I had a neat portfolio to point at. We were working on a media concept together. Travel, food, real human texture. Not the polished tourism stuff. The working title was a series built around local eating: أكل شوارع وبيوت (street food and home food). The idea was to create episodes with enough flavor and narrative weight to stand as a real series. He asked me to direct a trip to Zanzibar. Move fast. Film well. Shape something coherent from real life. It wasn't just production. It was making leadership decisions on the go. In the car, in the street, with time and people moving around you. The work hasn't been published yet. Life and timing got in the way. But the trust behind it is the whole point.

"اكتب أي شيء… وأنا أوقع عليه."

- محمد سلامة (كاتب وفنان)

"Write anything... and I'll sign it."

- Mohammed Salama, writer, artist.
