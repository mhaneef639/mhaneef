Why I’m Starting With Clarity Sessions

The thing I kept doing for free — again and again — was helping people slow down long enough to see what’s actually in front of them.

AlUla, 2025. Long exposure. The place where I first found clarity. In a dark enough sky, you start seeing what was always there. Original work.

I spent months trying to figure out what to launch first.

I can do a lot of things. Video production, creative direction, consulting, building systems. I kept going back and forth between offers, funnels, packages. The usual startup overthinking.

Then I noticed something. The thing I kept doing for free, in conversations with friends, with other founders, with people who’d message me out of nowhere, was helping them think.

Not giving advice. Not coaching them up. Just helping them slow down long enough to see what was actually in front of them.

Here’s what I’ve learned from living inside very different worlds.

Medicine, entrepreneurship, creative work, moving between Saudi Arabia and abroad, building things from scratch more than once.

Most people who feel stuck aren’t stupid. They’re not lazy. They’re overwhelmed.

They have too many options and not enough clarity about which ones actually matter right now. They’re reacting to pressure instead of responding to reality. And the longer that goes on, the worse the decisions get.

I’ve been there. After I left medicine and quit my day job, there were stretches where I had the skills, the ideas, and the drive but I couldn’t see clearly. Too much noise. Too many directions. Not enough stillness to actually think.

What got me through wasn’t motivation or hustle advice. It was sitting down, slowing things down, and being honest about what was true. Not what I wished was true.

That’s what a Clarity Session is.

Thirty minutes. You and me. No fluff.

We slow things down. We name what’s actually going on. Not the story you’ve been telling yourself, but the real situation. We lay out the tradeoffs honestly. And we find a direction you can actually commit to, not just one that sounds good.

It’s not therapy. It’s not motivational coaching. It’s not me pretending I have your answers.

It’s structured thinking with someone who’s been through enough disruption to know that clarity is the thing that makes everything else possible.

I could have launched with a dozen different services. But I’ve learned, partly the hard way, that starting with what’s most needed beats starting with what’s most impressive.

Right now, with everything shifting. AI changing how work happens, entire career paths dissolving, financial pressure building. The most useful thing I can offer someone isn’t another product.

It’s thirty minutes of actual clarity.

If you’re sitting with a decision that feels heavier than it should, or you’re stuck between directions and can’t see which one is real, book a session. I’d be glad to think it through with you.
