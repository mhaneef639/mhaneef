About Me

A summary of my journey, and what guides me forward.

Hello, I'm Mohammed

I love helping people find clarity, especially in moments of confusion. When someone feels a decision is hard, the noise is high, and they don't know how to act — that's the moment they need the clarity I help them reach.

My background looks unusual at first glance, but the pattern becomes clear on reflection. I've worked across multiple systems: medicine, community, arts, athletics, and entrepreneurship. I studied and practiced medicine for a period, spending long stretches in high-pressure environments that demanded good judgment and decision-making under confusion — emergency departments and similar settings. I left medicine and returned to public life to pursue real experience and see what actually helps people improve their performance and make better decisions.

Each of these overlapping systems gave me a different angle on things related to pressure, identity, and discipline — and on the larger goals behind what we do.

And that unusual mix is precisely what makes the work I do simple: we calm down, slow movement, observe reality and name things clearly, define our priorities and what we're willing to trade, and choose the path we want to commit to.

My approach is practical and reflective at the same time. I'm not interested in noise, trends, or motivation for its own sake. I care about sound structure, taking responsibility, and alignment of values over time. What holds up when things get difficult? What actually works? That's what matters to me.

This space is for those who value depth, clarity, and honest work. If you're here to think clearly, build with clear structure and sound purpose, you're welcome here.

My Journey

2017

Foundations in Medicine

I began my professional path as a physician, where responsibility and structure shaped how I think. That experience gave me a deep understanding of living systems from a disciplined, defined perspective. Over time I also discovered the limits of rigid systems — and found they were holding me back from completing my mission.

2024

Exploration: Relearning, Rebuilding

I stepped outside the traditional path to touch reality myself — to see how humans actually function, far from theory. Through athletics, creative work, and direct experience, I refined my understanding of performance under pressure, resilience, communication, expression, and creating real impact.

2026

Where I Am Today

Today I work across three interconnected paths: Clarity Sessions, creative work, and building Cosmic League. My work starts from design built on clarity and simplicity — in how we live, work, and create — while building effective and sustainable systems.
