About Me

My story, journey, and what drives me forward.

Your Photo

Hello, I'm Mohammed

work as a creative systems thinker at the intersection of performance, consciousness, and design. At the core of everything I do is a simple belief: clarity matters. How we live, work, and create depends on how clearly we see ourselves and the systems around us.

My path hasn’t been a straight line. I was trained in medicine and spent years inside structured, high-pressure systems. Over time, I felt a growing gap between theory and real human experience. That pushed me to step away and explore how people actually function, physically, mentally, and socially, outside rigid frameworks. Along the way, I became deeply involved in athletics, creative work, and long-form thinking. I taught myself photography and video as a way to express ideas visually. I trained my body to understand discipline and resilience firsthand. And I kept returning to the same question: what actually helps humans grow in a sustainable and honest way? Today, my work lives in three areas. I offer advisory work to individuals and organizations around performance, leadership, and decision-making. I do creative work through photography and video, not just as content, but as a way to communicate meaning and identity. And I’m building Cosmic League, a long-term community and venture focused on human potential, connection, and conscious systems. My approach is practical and reflective. I’m not interested in hype, shortcuts, or motivation for its own sake. I care about structure, responsibility, and alignment over time. What holds up when things get difficult. What actually works in real life.

This space is for people who value depth, clarity, and honest work. If you’re here to think clearly, create intentionally, or build something meaningful, you’re welcome here.
