Frequently Asked Questions

Is this therapy or medical/legal/financial advice?

No. This is a Clarity Session focused on decision-making, structure, and next steps. It is not medical, legal, or financial advice.

What should I bring to the session?

Nothing extra. Just arrive with a clear head and be ready to speak honestly. The intake form covers the context I need (what you're facing, what you've tried, constraints, and what "a win" looks like).

What do I receive after the session?

A short document emailed to you: a summary, the key framing, the tradeoffs we identified, and a practical next-step plan.

Do you also execute (creative work / production)?

Not by default. Execution or creative production only happens if we align after clarity is established, and it requires a separate agreement (scope, timeline, pricing).

What if we're not aligned?

If we discover misalignment, we'll be transparent. Future work may be declined or renegotiated to fit reality.
